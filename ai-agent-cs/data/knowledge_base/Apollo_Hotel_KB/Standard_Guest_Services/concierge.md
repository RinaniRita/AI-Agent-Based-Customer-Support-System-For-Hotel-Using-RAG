# Concierge Services

## Overview

Our dedicated concierge team is available around the clock to assist guests with dining, entertainment, and travel arrangements to elevate their luxury stay.

## Desk Hours

- Availability: 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

## Services Provided

- Dining reservations (internal and external).
- Transportation booking and private cars.
- Local event tickets and guided tours.
- General area inquiries and recommendations.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest requests a high-value or complex booking (e.g., private jet charter, VIP tickets).
- The guest needs immediate emergency assistance.
- The request requires external vendor coordination that the AI cannot perform automatically.
