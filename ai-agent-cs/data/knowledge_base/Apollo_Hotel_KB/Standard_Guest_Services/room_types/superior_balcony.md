# Superior Room with Balcony

## Description
The Superior Balcony room at **Apollo Hotel** features a private outdoor area where guests can enjoy the fresh air and views of **Hanoi**. This room combines comfort with the luxury of a private outdoor space.

## Pricing
- **Base Price:** Starting from 235 EUR per night for 1 adult (prices vary by date and availability). Breakfast is included.

## Room Details
- **Bed Configuration:** Double bed or twin beds
- **Maximum Occupancy:** 2 people
- **View:** City view over rooftops
- **Balcony:** Yes
- **Bathroom:** Bath or shower, heated floor

## Amenities
- Individually controlled air conditioning
- Smart TV with multilingual channels and free Wi-Fi
- Minibar, safe, hairdryer
- Kettle and coffee/tea making set

## Special Benefits
- Complimentary bottle of water and sweet treat upon arrival
- Nespresso coffee machine
- Bathrobes and slippers

## Policies & Restrictions
- **Children:** Not suitable to accommodate children under 6 years old.
- **Extra Beds / Cots:** It is not possible to place a baby cot or an extra bed in this room.
- **Pets:** Not allowed.
