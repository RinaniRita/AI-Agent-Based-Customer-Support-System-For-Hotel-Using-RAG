# Printing and Office Services

## Overview

Our business center provides essential document preparation services, including high-quality printing, copying, and faxing.

## Printing and Copying

- Black and White: $0.10 per page.
- Color: $0.50 per page.
- Wireless Printing: Guests can print securely from personal devices directly to the business center printers.

## Additional Services

- Scanning: Complimentary scanning to email or USB drives.
- Faxing: $1.00 per page for domestic faxes; $3.00 per page for international faxes.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- The printer runs out of ink or paper during an urgent guest print job.
- A guest disputes the final charges for a large printing or faxing batch.
- A guest reports a security concern (e.g., highly confidential documents left on the machine).
