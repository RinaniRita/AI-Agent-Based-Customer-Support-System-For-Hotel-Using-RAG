# Business Center Services

## Overview

Our business center provides a quiet, fully equipped workspace designed to meet the productivity needs of our corporate guests.

## Access and Hours

- Hours: Open 24/7.
- Access: Requires a valid guest room keycard for entry.

## Amenities

- Workstations: Both Windows and Mac computers are available.
- Internet: Complimentary high-speed wired and wireless internet access.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest reports a broken computer or network outage.
- A non-registered guest is attempting to use the business center.
- A guest requires immediate IT support for a personal device connection.
