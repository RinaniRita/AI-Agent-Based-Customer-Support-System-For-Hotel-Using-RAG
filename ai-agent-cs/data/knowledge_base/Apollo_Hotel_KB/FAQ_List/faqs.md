# Apollo Hotel Scripted FAQs

## Overview
This document contains meticulously crafted, scripted Q&A pairs Tailored exclusively for **Apollo Hotel**. When a guest asks a question matching the intent below, the AI assistant must use this information to provide a highly professional, accurate, and perfectly formatted response.

### **Q: What are the full policies of the hotel? / What are your house rules?**
**Scripted Response:**
Here is a comprehensive overview of the official House Rules at Apollo Hotel:
* 🕒 **Check-In/Out:** Check-in begins at 14:00 (2:00 PM), and check-out is by 11:00 AM.
* 🐶 **Pets:** We maintain a strict **No Pets** policy. Accommodation will be refused without refund for guests arriving with pets.
* 🚭 **Smoking:** The entire hotel interior is strictly 100% smoke-free (including e-cigarettes). A €250 penalty applies for violations.
* 🤫 **Quiet Hours:** Observed daily from 22:00 (10:00 PM) to 6:00 AM for the comfort of all guests.
* 🌍 **Sustainability:** We proudly participate in eco-friendly practices, including waste separation and towel reuse.

### **Q: What time is check-in and check-out? Can I request a late check-out?**
**Scripted Response:**
Our standard check-in time is **14:00 (2:00 PM)** and check-out is at **11:00 AM**. 
If you require a late check-out, please contact the reception in advance. It is subject to availability and generally incurs an additional fee.

### **Q: What time is breakfast? / Do you have gluten-free or early breakfast?**
**Scripted Response:**
Our rich breakfast buffet is served daily from **6:30 AM to 10:30 AM**, featuring Continental, Full English, and Vegetarian options!
* **Dietary Needs:** We happily provide gluten-free baskets and lactose-free options upon request (fees may apply).
* **Early Departure:** If you are leaving before 6:30 AM, please notify the front desk the day prior, and we will prepare an **early breakfast box** for you!

### **Q: Do you have parking? How much is it?**
**Scripted Response:**
Yes, we offer secure underground garage parking for **€35 per night**. 
* **Crucial Note:** Spaces are limited and **must be reserved in advance**. 
* **Vehicle Restrictions:** For safety and structural reasons, our garage **strictly prohibits** LPG, CNG, Electric, and Hybrid vehicles. Minor size and weight limits also apply.

### **Q: Are pets allowed at the hotel?**
**Scripted Response:**
We apologize for any inconvenience, but Apollo Hotel maintains a strict **No Pets** policy. Please note that arriving with a pet will result in the refusal of accommodation without a refund, per our official House Rules.

### **Q: Do you allow smoking?**
**Scripted Response:**
No, Apollo Hotel is a **strictly 100% non-smoking** property (this includes all e-cigarettes and vapes). If evidence of smoking is found indoors, a deep-cleaning penalty of **€250** will be charged to the room.
