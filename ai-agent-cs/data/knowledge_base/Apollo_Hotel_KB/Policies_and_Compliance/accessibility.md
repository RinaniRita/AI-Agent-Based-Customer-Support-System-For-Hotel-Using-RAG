# Accessibility Policy

## Overview

We are committed to providing an inclusive, welcoming, and fully accessible luxury environment for all guests, compliant with all ADA standards.

## Physical Access

- Facilities: The main entrance, lobby, restaurants, and designated guest rooms are fully accessible.
- Mobility Equipment: Complimentary wheelchairs are available at the bell desk on a first-come, first-served basis.

## Sensory Accommodations

- Visual/Hearing Aids: TTY equipment and visual alarm devices are available upon request at the time of booking or at check-in.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest arrives and their guaranteed accessible room is unavailable.
- The guest requires specialized, high-care medical equipment rentals.
- A guest files a formal complaint regarding discrimination or lack of accessibility.
