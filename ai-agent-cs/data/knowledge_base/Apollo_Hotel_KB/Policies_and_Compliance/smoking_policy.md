# Smoking Policy

## Overview

To maintain a pristine and healthy luxury environment, our property strictly enforces a comprehensive indoor smoke-free policy.

## Smoke-Free Environment

- Rule: We are a 100% smoke-free property indoors. This includes cigarettes, cigars, e-cigarettes, and vaping devices.
- Designated Areas: Smoking is only permitted in explicitly marked outdoor designated smoking areas.

## Violations

- Cleaning Fee: A strict $500 deep-cleaning fee will be charged to the guest's account if evidence of smoking is found inside any guest room or suite.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest aggressively disputes the $500 smoking fee applied to their bill.
- A guest repeatedly violates the policy after receiving formal warnings.
- A guest complains about severe secondhand smoke entering their room from a neighboring balcony or hallway.
