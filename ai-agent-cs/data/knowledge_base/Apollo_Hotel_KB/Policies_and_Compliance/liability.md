# Liability Policy

## Overview

These guidelines outline the responsibilities regarding personal property and hotel assets to ensure mutual protection and understanding during a stay.

## Personal Property

- In-Room Safes: Guests are strongly encouraged to use the complimentary electronic safe provided in each room for valuables.
- Disclaimer: The hotel is not liable for the loss, theft, or damage of personal items left unsecured in guest rooms or public areas.

## Hotel Property

- Damage: Guests are financially responsible for any damages caused to hotel property, furnishings, or equipment during their stay.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest claims high-value items (jewelry, electronics) were stolen from their room.
- Major property damage occurs (e.g., a broken window, flooded bathroom, or fire).
- A guest explicitly refuses to pay for documented damages charged to their folio.
