# Child Safety and Amenities

## Overview

The safety and comfort of our family guests is a top priority. We provide specialized amenities to childproof rooms and ensure a secure environment.

## In-Room Amenities

- Available Gear: Complimentary cribs, rollaway beds, and diaper disposal bins are available 24/7 upon request.
- Childproofing: Electrical outlet covers and sharp-corner guards can be installed in your room prior to arrival by our housekeeping team.

## Pool and Facility Safety

- Supervision: Children under 12 must be accompanied and actively supervised by an adult at all times in the pool and fitness areas.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest reports a lost or missing child on the hotel property.
- An injury occurs in the pool or fitness area involving a minor.
- A guest reports that a provided crib or rollaway bed is broken or unsafe.
