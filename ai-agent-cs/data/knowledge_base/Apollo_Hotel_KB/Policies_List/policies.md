# Apollo Hotel Policies & House Rules

## Overview
This document outlines the strict official policies of Apollo Hotel. All AI responses must strictly adhere to these rules. Do not invent policies.

## 1. Arrival & Departure
* **Check-In Time**: Standard check-in begins at **14:00 (2:00 PM)**.
* **Check-Out Time**: Standard check-out must be completed by **11:00 AM**.
* **Late Check-Out**: Late check-out is subject to availability, must be agreed upon with reception in advance, and incurs an additional fee.
* **Minors**: For guests arriving with children under 18, official documentation confirming the relationship is required.

## 2. Pet Policy
* **STRICTLY NO PETS**: Pets are **not** allowed at Apollo Hotel under any circumstances.
* **Penalty**: The hotel reserves the right to refuse accommodation without a refund if a guest arrives with a pet.

## 3. Smoking & Conduct
* **100% Smoke-Free**: The entire hotel interior is strictly non-smoking (this includes e-cigarettes and vaping).
* **Penalty**: A severe penalty of **€250** will be charged for any violation of the smoking policy.
* **Quiet Hours**: Guests must observe quiet hours from **22:00 (10:00 PM) to 6:00 AM** to ensure a peaceful environment for all.

## 4. Sustainability Policy
Apollo Hotel is committed to environmental protection. Our sustainability practices include:
* Strict waste separation and recycling protocols.
* Encouraging towel and bedding reuse to save water.
* Using eco-friendly cleaning products throughout the property.
* Active programs designed to reduce food waste.

## When AI Should Escalate
The AI assistant must trigger a human escalation if:
* A guest attempts to argue the strict No Pets policy or requests a refund after being denied entry.
* A guest disputes the €250 smoking penalty.
* A guest makes a serious noise complaint during quiet hours.
