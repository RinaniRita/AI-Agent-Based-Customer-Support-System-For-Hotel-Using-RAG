# Visa and Travel Documentation

## Overview

While we cannot legally process or sponsor visas, we provide administrative support and official documentation to assist guests with their travel requirements.

## Extension Assistance

- Support: Our business center provides free printing and copying for all travel documents.
- Documentation: Guests needing proof of accommodation for visa purposes can request an official, stamped reservation confirmation letter from the front desk.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest explicitly requests that the hotel officially sponsor their visa application.
- The guest's visa has expired and they are seeking emergency legal or embassy assistance.
- A guest demands a refund or disputes a cancellation fee because their travel visa was denied.
