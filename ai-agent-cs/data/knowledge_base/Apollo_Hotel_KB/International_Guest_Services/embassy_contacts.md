# Embassy and Consulate Contacts

## Overview

To assist our international guests during emergencies or administrative needs, we maintain an up-to-date directory of local embassies and consulates.

## Directory Access

- Availability: A comprehensive directory of local embassies and consulates is available 24/7 at the concierge desk.
- Assistance: The concierge can assist with arranging priority transportation to any local embassy.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest has lost their passport and requires immediate embassy intervention.
- The guest reports a legal or political emergency involving their home country.
- A guest requires complex translation assistance while speaking with their embassy.
