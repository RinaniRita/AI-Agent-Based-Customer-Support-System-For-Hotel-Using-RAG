# Loyalty Program

## Overview

Our loyalty program rewards frequent guests with exclusive perks, points on every stay, and prioritized services.

## Tiers and Earning

- Tiers: Silver, Gold, Platinum.
- Earning: Guests earn 10 points per $1 spent on standard room rates.

## Standard Benefits

- Perks: Complimentary premium Wi-Fi and priority early check-in (subject to availability).
- Platinum Perks: Includes access to the Executive Lounge.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest is missing points from a previous stay and demands an account audit.
- The guest requests an unearned complimentary tier upgrade.
- The guest wants to redeem points for an immediate on-site purchase requiring manual approval.
