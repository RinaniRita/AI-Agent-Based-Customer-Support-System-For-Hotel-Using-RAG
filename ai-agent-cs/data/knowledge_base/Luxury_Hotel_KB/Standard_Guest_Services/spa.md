# Spa Services

## Overview

Our luxury on-site spa provides a tranquil environment with a full menu of massages, facials, and wellness treatments.

## Hours and Booking

- Operating Hours: 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM daily.
- Reservations: Advance booking is highly recommended and required at least 24 hours prior to the appointment.

## Cancellation

- Policy: A 12-hour notice is required to cancel or reschedule a treatment.
- Penalty: Cancellations within 12 hours incur a 50% cancellation fee.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest disputes a spa cancellation fee.
- The guest reports a severe medical condition or allergy prior to a treatment.
- A guest wants to book a couple's treatment or specific therapist when the schedule is fully booked.
