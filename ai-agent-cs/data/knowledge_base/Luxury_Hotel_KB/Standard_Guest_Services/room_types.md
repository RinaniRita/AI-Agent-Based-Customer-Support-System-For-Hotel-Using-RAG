# Room Types and Occupancy

## Overview

We offer a variety of luxurious accommodations designed for comfort and elegance. Strict occupancy limits are enforced for safety and fire code compliance.

## Standard Rooms

- Configurations: Single King or Double Queen beds.
- Maximum Occupancy: 4 guests per room.

## Suites

- Configurations: Executive Suite featuring a separate living area and premium amenities.
- Maximum Occupancy: 6 guests per suite.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest requests a complimentary room upgrade.
- The guest has more people in their party than the maximum occupancy allows.
- A guest complains about the size, view, or specific location of their assigned room.
