# Technology and Wi-Fi

## Overview

We provide seamless connectivity and modern in-room entertainment options to enhance the guest experience.

## Internet Access

- Standard Wi-Fi: Complimentary for all registered guests.
- Premium Wi-Fi: $15 per day (Complimentary for Gold and Platinum loyalty members). Ideal for streaming and large downloads.

## In-Room Tech

- Features: Smart TVs with personal streaming capabilities (Netflix, Hulu) and bedside USB/USB-C charging ports.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest experiences a complete loss of internet connectivity.
- The guest requires advanced technical support to connect a corporate VPN.
- In-room technology (Smart TV, electronic blinds, or thermostat) malfunctions.
