# Reservation Rules

## Overview

To guarantee accommodation and uphold our luxury standards, we have specific requirements for securing a room reservation.

## Booking Requirements

- Age Minimum: Guests must be at least 18 years old to book a room and check in.
- Guarantee: A valid credit card is required to secure and guarantee all reservations at the time of booking.

## Modifications

- Changes: Modifications to reservations can be made up to 48 hours prior to arrival, subject to availability and rate changes.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- An underage guest is attempting to book or check in.
- A guest booked through a third-party site (like Expedia or Booking.com) and is demanding a refund directly from the hotel.
- The hotel is in an overbooked situation and a guest's reservation needs to be relocated.
