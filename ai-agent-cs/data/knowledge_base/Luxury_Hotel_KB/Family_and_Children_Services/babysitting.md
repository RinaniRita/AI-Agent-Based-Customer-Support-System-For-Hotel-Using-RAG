# Babysitting Services

## Overview

We partner with trusted, licensed professionals to provide reliable childcare services, allowing parents to enjoy an evening out or attend to business with peace of mind.

## Booking and Rates

- Notice: A 24-hour advance notice is required to arrange childcare.
- Rates: $25 per hour, requiring a 3-hour minimum booking.

## Care Providers

- Qualifications: All babysitters are bonded, CPR-certified, and sourced through a rigorously vetted third-party agency.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest reports a medical emergency or injury involving a child and the babysitter.
- The scheduled babysitter fails to arrive at the designated time.
- A guest requests emergency, immediate childcare without the 24-hour notice.
