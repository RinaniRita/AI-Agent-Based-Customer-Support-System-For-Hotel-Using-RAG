# Data Privacy and GDPR Compliance

## Overview

We strictly adhere to global data protection regulations to ensure that our guests' personal and financial information remains highly secure and confidential.

## Data Collection

- Usage: Guest information is collected strictly for reservation management, billing, and providing personalized luxury services.
- Security: All personal and financial data is encrypted and stored on secure, access-restricted servers.

## Guest Rights

- Control: Guests have the right to request access to, modification of, or deletion of their personal data by contacting the privacy officer.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest suspects a data breach, credit card fraud, or identity theft linked to the hotel.
- Law enforcement or a third party requests access to a specific guest's records.
- A guest demands immediate, permanent deletion of all their records while currently on-property.
