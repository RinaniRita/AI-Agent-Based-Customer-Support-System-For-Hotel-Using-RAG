# Shopping and Retail

## Overview

We provide access to exclusive on-site boutiques and recommendations for the city's premier shopping districts to satisfy our guests' retail needs.

## On-Site Boutiques

- Offerings: Luxury sundries, travel essentials, and designer apparel are available in the lobby gift shop.
- Hours: Open daily from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

## Local Shopping Districts

- Malls: The upscale Premier Fashion Mall is a 10-minute drive away via our complimentary hotel shuttle.
- Markets: A local artisan market operates every weekend morning in the nearby plaza.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest claims an item purchased from the on-site boutique is defective and demands a refund outside of standard policy.
- The guest requests a dedicated personal shopper for high-end luxury goods.
- A guest asks the hotel to act as a proxy to purchase and ship restricted or highly valuable luxury items.
