# Apollo Hotel - Most Frequently Asked Questions

## 1. What time is Breakfast and where is it located?
Our complimentary hot buffet breakfast is served daily at the ground-floor *Lumina Restaurant*. 
- Weekdays: 6:30 AM to 10:30 AM
- Weekends: 6:30 AM to 11:00 AM

## 2. Where are the Gym, Pool, and Spa?
- **Fitness Center:** Located on the 2nd floor, open 24/7 with your room key.
- **Rooftop Infinity Pool and Spa:** Located on the top floor, open daily from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

## 3. Do you offer Luggage Storage?
Yes! We offer complimentary, secure luggage storage at the front desk. You may store your bags before check-in or after check-out on your day of departure.

## 4. Is the tap water safe to drink?
Yes, the tap water in the hotel is completely safe to drink. In addition, we provide two complimentary bottles of still water in your room daily.

## 5. Do you have universal power adapters or phone chargers?
Yes, universal power adapters and phone charging cables are available to borrow at the Front Desk free of charge.

## 6. Do you have an iron or offer laundry service?
- **Ironing:** An iron and ironing board are located in your room's wardrobe.
- **Laundry/Dry Cleaning:** We offer same-day laundry and dry cleaning. Please place your items in the provided bag and notify the Front Desk before 9:00 AM.

## 7. How do I use the in-room safe?
Your safe is located in the closet. 
- **To lock:** Close the door, enter a 4-digit code of your choice, and press LOCK. 
- **To unlock:** Enter the identical 4-digit code.

## 8. What are the options for Airport Transportation?
- **Private Transfer:** We offer private airport transfers directly to and from the hotel for a flat rate of €45.
- **Train:** The Airport Express Train station is located just a 10-minute walk from our lobby.
- **Taxi:** The front desk can arrange a trusted taxi for you at any time.

## 9. Can I get a wake-up call?
Absolutely! Inform our Front Desk or the automated system via the Telegram bot with the exact time you would like to be woken up, and we will call your room.
