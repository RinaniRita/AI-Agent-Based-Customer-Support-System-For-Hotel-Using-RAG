# Apollo Hotel Scripted FAQs

## Overview
This document contains meticulously crafted, scripted Q&A pairs tailored exclusively for **Apollo Hotel**. When a guest asks a question matching the intent below, the AI assistant must use this information to provide a highly professional, accurate, and perfectly formatted response.

### **Q: What are the full policies of the hotel? / What are your house rules?**
**Scripted Response:**
Here is a comprehensive overview of the official House Rules at Apollo Hotel:
* 🕒 **Check-In/Out:** Check-in begins at 14:00 (2:00 PM), and check-out is by 11:00 AM.
* 🐶 **Pets:** We maintain a strict **No Pets** policy to ensure the comfort of all guests.
* 🚭 **Smoking:** The entire hotel interior is strictly 100% smoke-free. A penalty of **€250** applies for violations.
* 🤫 **Quiet Hours:** Observed daily from 22:00 (10:00 PM) to 6:00 AM.
* 🌍 **Sustainability:** We participate in eco-friendly practices including waste separation and towel reuse.

### **Q: What time is check-in and check-out? Can I request a late check-out?**
**Scripted Response:**
Our standard check-in time is **14:00 (2:00 PM)** and check-out is at **11:00 AM**. 
* **Late Check-out:** We offer a complimentary 30-minute grace period. For extensions beyond 11:30 AM, please contact reception. Fees apply and it is subject to availability.
* **Luggage:** We offer complimentary secure luggage storage if you arrive early or wish to explore after checking out!

### **Q: What time is breakfast? / Do you have gluten-free or early breakfast?**
**Scripted Response:**
Our rich breakfast buffet is served daily from **6:30 AM to 10:30 AM**.
* **Dietary Needs:** We provide gluten-free and lactose-free options upon request.
* **Early Departure:** If you leave before 6:30 AM, please notify the front desk by 8:00 PM the night prior for an **early breakfast box**.

### **Q: Do you have parking? How much is it?**
**Scripted Response:**
Yes, we offer secure underground garage parking for **€35 per night**. 
* **EV & Hybrid Vehicles:** We warmly welcome Electric and Hybrid vehicles! We also have **EV Charging Stations** available for guest use in the garage.
* **Reservations:** Spaces are limited, so we recommend reserving your spot in advance.

### **Q: What is the Wi-Fi password?**
**Scripted Response:**
We offer two tiers of high-speed connectivity:
* **Standard Wi-Fi:** Complimentary for all guests. Simply connect to **'Apollo_Guest'** (no password required).
* **Premium Wi-Fi:** Complimentary for checked-in guests. Your dedicated network name and password are printed directly on your **hotel key card**.
