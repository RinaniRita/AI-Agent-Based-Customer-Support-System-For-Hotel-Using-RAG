# Housekeeping Services

## Overview

We provide pristine room conditions through daily cleaning and evening turndown services, ensuring a comfortable and luxurious environment.

## Daily Cleaning

- Hours: 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM daily.
- Privacy: Place the "Do Not Disturb" sign on your door to decline service.

## Extra Amenities

- Requests: Extra towels, blankets, and toiletries are available 24/7 via the front desk.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest reports an urgent spill, stain, or cleanliness issue.
- The guest claims an item is missing from their room after cleaning.
- A guest requests a deep cleaning due to severe allergies.
