The Junior Suite offers extra space and is beautifully furnished in light natural colors. Located at the very end of the corridor, it is one of our quietest rooms at **Apollo Hotel**. It is the perfect choice to relax after a long day of exploring **Hanoi** or working at the office.

## Pricing
- **Base Price:** Starting from 175 EUR per night for 1 adult (prices vary by date and availability). Breakfast is included.

## Room Details
- **Bed Configuration:** 1 Double bed (cannot be separated). The comfortable sofa can be unfolded to accommodate a 3rd adult or 2 small children (up to 12 years).
- **Maximum Occupancy:** 3 adults OR 2 adults and 2 children
- **View:** No specific view
- **Bathroom:** Bathtub with shower and underfloor heating

## Amenities
- Spacious working desk and fast free Wi-Fi
- Smart TV with multilingual selection
- Individually controlled air conditioning
- Minibar, safe, hairdryer
- Kettle with coffee and tea making set

## Special Benefits
- Nespresso coffee machine
- Bathrobes and slippers

## Policies & Restrictions
- **Children:** Allowed (including children under 6 years old).
- **Extra Beds / Cots:** A baby cot can be added to the room.
