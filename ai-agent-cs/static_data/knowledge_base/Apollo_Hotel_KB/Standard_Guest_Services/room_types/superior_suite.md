# Superior Suite

## Description
The Superior Suite is the largest and most luxurious room type at **Apollo Hotel**. Located on the high floor corner, it features a large bedroom and a separate living room with a dining area. It is the perfect choice for families or guests who want to enjoy the best of **Hanoi**.

## Pricing
- **Base Price:** Starting from 235 EUR per night for 1 adult (prices vary by date and availability). Breakfast is included.

## Room Details
- **Bed Configuration:** King bed. The living room sofa can be converted into a full-size double bed for 2 extra people.
- **Maximum Occupancy:** Up to 4 people
- **View:** Courtyard
- **Bathrooms:** 2 full bathrooms with bath and heated floors

## Amenities & Features
- 2 working desks and comfortable seating
- 2 sets of smart TVs with multilingual channels
- Individually controlled air conditioning and free Wi-Fi
- Minibar, safe, hairdryer
- Microwave and coffee/tea making facilities

## Special Benefits
- Complimentary bottle of water and sweet treat upon arrival
- Nespresso coffee machine
- Bathrobes and slippers
- Early check-in and late check-out (subject to availability)

## Policies & Restrictions
- **Children:** allowed (including children under 6 years old).
- **Extra Beds / Cots:** Baby cot is allowed.
- Families welcome.
