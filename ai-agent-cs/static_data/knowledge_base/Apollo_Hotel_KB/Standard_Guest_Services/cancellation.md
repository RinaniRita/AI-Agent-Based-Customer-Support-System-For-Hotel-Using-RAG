# Reservation Cancellation Policy

## Overview

Our hotel offers flexible and transparent cancellation options. We require timely notice to avoid penalties and accommodate other guests.

## Standard Rates

- Deadline: Cancellations must be made 48 hours prior to the 3:00 PM check-in time.
- Penalty: Cancellations after the deadline incur a fee of one night's room rate plus taxes.

## Non-Refundable Rates

- Policy: Full payment is required at booking.
- Refunds: No refunds are issued for cancellations on these rates.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest requests a penalty waiver due to medical or travel emergencies.
- The guest disputes a cancellation fee already charged.
- The guest expresses anger over the non-refundable policy.
