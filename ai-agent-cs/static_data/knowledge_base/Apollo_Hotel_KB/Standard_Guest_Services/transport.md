# Transportation Services

## Overview

We offer convenient and reliable transportation options, including a complimentary airport shuttle and premium car services, to ensure smooth travel.

## Airport Shuttle

- Schedule: Departs the hotel every hour on the hour from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM.
- Cost: Complimentary for registered hotel guests.

## Car Services

- Booking: Private town cars and limousines are available through the concierge desk for an additional fee.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest misses a flight due to a delayed or full hotel shuttle.
- The guest requires a specialized ADA-accessible vehicle for transport.
- A guest leaves a valuable item in a private car or the hotel shuttle.
