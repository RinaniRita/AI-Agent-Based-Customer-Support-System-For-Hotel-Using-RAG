# Payment Policies

## Overview

Our payment policies ensure a secure and transparent billing process for all guests. We require a valid payment method on file for the duration of the stay.

## Accepted Methods

- Credit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover.
- Cash: Cash is accepted at check-out, but a credit card is still required at check-in for incidentals.

## Incidentals Hold

- Hold Amount: A €50 per night hold is authorized on the credit card at check-in.
- Release: Unused funds are released upon check-out and may take 3-5 business days to process depending on the bank.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest's credit card is declined and they cannot provide an alternative.
- The guest disputes a specific charge on their final folio.
- A guest insists on paying the initial deposit entirely in cash without a card on file.
