# Check-In and Check-Out Policy

## Overview

We aim to provide a seamless arrival and departure experience. Standard timings are enforced to ensure rooms are perfectly prepared for incoming guests.

## Check-In

- Standard Time: 3:00 PM.
- Requirements: Valid government-issued photo ID and a valid credit card.
- Early Check-In: Subject to availability; not guaranteed unless booked for the prior night.

## Check-Out

- Standard Time: 12:00 PM.
- Late Check-Out (up to 3:00 PM): Incurs a €20 per hour fee (subject to availability).
- Late Check-Out (after 3:00 PM): Incurs a full night's charge.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest demands a guaranteed early check-in or late check-out without fees.
- The guest is experiencing issues with mobile key or self-check-in kiosks.
- A guest disputes late check-out charges.
