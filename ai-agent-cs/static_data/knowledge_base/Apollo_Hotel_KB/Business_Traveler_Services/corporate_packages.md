# Corporate Packages

## Overview

We offer specialized rates and tailored amenities for business travelers to ensure a productive and seamless stay.

## Eligibility and Rates

- Requirement: Guests must provide a valid corporate ID or business card at check-in.
- Discount: Eligible guests receive up to 15% off the standard daily room rate.

## Package Inclusions

- Perks: Complimentary premium Wi-Fi, access to the daily breakfast buffet, and priority late check-out up to 2:00 PM.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest disputes the validity of their corporate ID and is denied the rate.
- The guest wants to retroactively apply a corporate rate to a past booking.
- There is a dispute regarding direct billing to a corporate account.
