# Courier and Shipping Services

## Overview

We facilitate reliable inbound and outbound shipping services to assist guests with important documents and packages.

## Outbound Shipping

- Providers: FedEx, UPS, and DHL pickups are scheduled daily at 3:00 PM.
- Supplies: Complimentary basic shipping envelopes and boxes are available at the concierge desk.

## Inbound Packages

- Receiving Fee: €5 per package up to 10 lbs; €10 for packages over 10 lbs.
- Storage: Packages can be securely held for up to 5 days prior to a guest's arrival.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest claims an expected high-value package is missing or lost.
- A package arrives visibly damaged.
- A guest requires an emergency, after-hours international shipment.
