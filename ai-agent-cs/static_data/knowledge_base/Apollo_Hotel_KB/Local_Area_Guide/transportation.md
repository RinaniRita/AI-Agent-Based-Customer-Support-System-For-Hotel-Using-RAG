# Transportation in Hanoi (2026)

## 📱 Ride-Hailing Apps (Recommended)
The safest and most transparent way to travel in Hanoi is via smartphone apps:
- **Grab:** The standard ride-hailing app for cars and motorbikes. You see the price upfront and can pay via credit card.
- **Xanh SM:** A premium, eco-friendly fleet of all-electric VinFast cars. They are quiet, professional, and highly recommended for luxury travelers.
- **Be:** A popular local alternative to Grab.

## 🚕 Traditional Taxis
If you hail a taxi on the street, stick to these reputable companies:
- **Mai Linh Taxi:** Easily recognizable by their green cars.
- **G7 Taxi:** Reliable silver-and-blue fleet.
- **Taxi Group:** Professional drivers often found at official hotel stands.

> [!WARNING]
> **Taxi Safety:** Always ensure the meter is turned on. Avoid unmarked "street taxis" or drivers who approach you aggressively at the airport.

## 🚶 Getting Around
- **Old Quarter:** Best explored on foot.
- **Rickshaws (Cyclos):** Great for a scenic tour, but agree on the price **before** you get in. Typical price is around 100,000 – 150,000 VND per hour.
