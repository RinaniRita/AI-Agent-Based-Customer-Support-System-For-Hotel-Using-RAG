# Apollo Hotel - Core Operating Policies

## Check-In and Check-Out Policy
- **Standard Check-In:** 2:00 PM (14:00)
- **Standard Check-Out:** 11:00 AM
- **Late Check-Out:** Guests may request a late check-out. It is strictly subject to availability on the day of departure. The late check-out fee is €20 per hour up until 3:00 PM.

## Deposit and Incidentals Policy
A €100 hold is placed on the guest's credit card at check-in to cover incidentals (minibar, room service, or damages). This hold is released immediately upon check-out, though it may take 3-5 business days to reflect on your bank statement.

## Strict Non-Smoking Policy
Apollo Hotel is a 100% non-smoking property. This includes smoking, e-cigarettes, and vaping in guest rooms, bathrooms, and on balconies. 
**Penalty:** A €250 deep-cleaning penalty will automatically be charged to the guest's folio if evidence of smoking is found.

## Visitor and Noise Policy
- **Visitors:** External visitors are welcome in the lobby and bar areas freely. For security reasons, external visitors are only allowed in guest rooms until 10:00 PM.
- **Quiet Hours:** Quiet hours are strictly enforced hotel-wide from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM to ensure all guests enjoy a peaceful stay.

## Pet Policy
We are a pet-free hotel. With the strict exception of certified service animals (with proper documentation required at check-in), no pets are allowed. Arriving with non-service animals will result in the immediate refusal of accommodation without refund.

## Lost & Found Policy
Any personal items left behind by guests are logged and kept securely by Housekeeping for 30 days. Perishable food, open beverages, and unsealed toiletries are discarded immediately upon checkout for hygiene reasons. Guests must pay shipping costs for any returned items.

## Package Delivery and Mail
We gladly accept packages on behalf of checked-in guests. 
- The package MUST be addressed to the exact Name registered on the booking. 
- We will send a Telegram notification to the guest when a package arrives at the Front Desk.
