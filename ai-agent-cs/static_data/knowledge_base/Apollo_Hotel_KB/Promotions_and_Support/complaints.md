# Guest Complaint Policy

## Overview

Our hotel is committed to delivering exceptional luxury hospitality. If a guest experiences dissatisfaction, we encourage immediate communication so that we can resolve the issue promptly and professionally.

## How Guests Can Submit a Complaint

Guests may submit complaints through:

- Front desk in person
- Hotel email support
- Telephone support line
- Online contact form
- AI chat assistant

## Types of Complaints

Common complaint categories include:

- Room cleanliness issues
- Noise disturbances
- Billing discrepancies
- Service delays
- Staff conduct concerns
- Facility malfunction

## Service Recovery Commitment

Upon receiving a complaint:

1. The issue is acknowledged immediately.
2. A responsible staff member is assigned.
3. A solution is proposed within a reasonable timeframe.
4. Appropriate compensation may be offered when justified.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- The guest expresses strong dissatisfaction or anger.
- Compensation is requested.
- Legal or safety concerns are mentioned.
- The issue remains unresolved after initial guidance.
