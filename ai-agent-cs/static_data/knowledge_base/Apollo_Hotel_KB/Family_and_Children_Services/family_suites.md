# Family Suites

## Overview

Our spacious Family Suites are designed to provide both togetherness and privacy, offering premium amenities tailored specifically for families traveling with children.

## Room Configuration

- Layout: Features two connecting rooms (one King bed room and one Double Queen room) connected by a private, shared foyer.
- Occupancy: Accommodates a maximum of 6 guests safely.

## Special Inclusions

- Welcome Amenities: Children 12 and under receive a complimentary activity book and a small plush toy at check-in.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- The internal connecting doors between the family suite rooms are jammed or malfunctioning.
- A guest demands to exceed the 6-person maximum occupancy for the suite.
- A family checks in and the promised children's welcome amenities were entirely omitted, causing guest frustration.
