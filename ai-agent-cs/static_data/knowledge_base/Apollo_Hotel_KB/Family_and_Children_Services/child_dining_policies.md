# Child Dining Policies

## Overview

We cater to our youngest guests with nutritious, kid-friendly dining options and family-oriented restaurant policies to ensure a stress-free mealtime.

## Children's Menu

- Offerings: Dedicated "Healthy Kids" menus featuring organic and allergy-friendly options are available at all on-site restaurants and via room service.

## Complimentary Dining

- Policy: Children under the age of 5 dine for free at the standard breakfast buffet when accompanied by a paying adult.

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A child experiences a severe allergic reaction to hotel food.
- A guest disputes a dining charge that they believe should have been complimentary for their child.
- A guest requires highly specialized infant formula or baby food not carried by the hotel.
