# Pet Policy

## Overview

We warmly welcome domestic pets under specific guidelines designed to ensure the comfort, safety, and pristine environment for all our guests.

## Allowed Pets and Fees

- Types: We welcome dogs and cats up to a maximum weight of 40 lbs.
- Fee: A non-refundable pet cleaning fee of €100 per stay applies. Maximum of two pets per room.

## Restrictions

- Public Areas: Pets must be leashed or in a carrier at all times in public spaces.
- Exclusions: Pets are strictly prohibited in the fitness center, pool area, and indoor dining venues (certified service animals excepted).

## When AI Should Escalate

The AI assistant must escalate if:

- A guest brings an unauthorized, exotic, or oversized pet onto the property.
- A pet bites, scratches, or injures another guest or staff member.
- There are continuous noise complaints (e.g., a barking dog) and the owner is unreachable.
